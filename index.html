About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my simple ePortfolio. Over the course of several years while employed in the world of hardware I noticed an upward trend and demand emerging in advanced software technologies. Within a 6-year period and while working fulltime, I was able to complete both a bachelors and a masters degree in computer science. Solving difficult problems and designing new tools through software will become a new and challenging endeavor which is something I am looking forward to.

Some of my passions on the outside include music production, motorcycle riding, and amateur radio
